FAQ's

Your POS Admin Questions, Answered

You can configure your store name, address, contact details, tax registration numbers, currency, and invoice branding directly from the admin dashboard. These settings are applied system-wide and automatically reflected on printed receipts, digital invoices, and financial reports.

The POS provides a fast and intuitive checkout experience with barcode scanning, quick product search, automatic tax calculation, discounts, and support for multiple payment methods such as cash, card, UPI, and split payments. This ensures faster billing and reduced queue time.

Admins can define product prices, tax slabs (GST/VAT), service charges, and rounding rules from the control panel. These rules are automatically applied during billing, ensuring accurate totals and compliance with regional tax regulations.

Inventory is updated in real time after every sale, return, or stock adjustment. The system tracks stock levels, generates low-stock alerts, and provides detailed inventory reports to prevent overstocking or stock shortages.

Yes, the POS allows you to create multiple staff roles such as cashier, manager, or admin. Permissions can be customized to control access to sales, refunds, reports, inventory, and settings, ensuring better security and accountability.
